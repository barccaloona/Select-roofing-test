What We Do

Expert Siding for Virginia Homes

Your home's siding is its first line of defense against Virginia's climate — from humid summers to icy winters. Select Roofing installs premium siding systems that combine superior protection with lasting beauty.

We work with all major siding materials and styles, helping you find the perfect balance of aesthetics, durability, and value for your home and budget.

Weatherproof
All siding systems are installed with proper moisture barriers and sealed edges for maximum protection.
Low Maintenance
Modern siding materials require minimal upkeep — no painting, no rot, no warping.
Energy Efficient
Insulated siding options can reduce energy costs by improving your home's thermal envelope.
Curb Appeal
New siding is one of the highest-ROI home improvements — averaging 76% return at resale.
Siding Services

What We Offer

Vinyl Siding

Versatile, affordable, and available in dozens of colors and profiles. Vinyl siding is virtually maintenance-free, fade-resistant, and designed to withstand Virginia's varied climate with ease.

Fiber Cement Siding

The durability of concrete with the look of real wood. James Hardie and similar fiber cement products resist rot, insects, and moisture — backed by a 30-year manufacturer warranty.

Wood & Composite

Natural cedar or engineered wood composites deliver timeless warmth and character. We properly treat, prime, and seal all wood products for maximum longevity in our humid climate.

Soffit, Fascia & Trim

Complete the exterior with professionally installed soffit, fascia, and trim work. Proper installation prevents moisture intrusion, promotes ventilation, and gives your home a polished, finished appearance.
