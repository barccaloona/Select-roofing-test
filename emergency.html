Emergency Roof Repair When You Need It Most

A damaged roof can turn a bad situation into a catastrophe within hours. Water intrusion causes mold, structural damage, and ruined belongings — every hour of delay multiplies the cost of repairs.

Select Roofing maintains an on-call emergency crew 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. When you call, a real person answers. Our team is deployed quickly with the materials and equipment needed to stabilize your roof immediately.

Emergency Services

What We Offer

Storm Damage Repair

Rapid response to wind, hail, and tornado damage. We assess structural integrity immediately and complete all necessary repairs to make your roof watertight and secure.

Emergency Tarping

Immediate professional tarping to protect your home interior from rain intrusion while permanent repairs are being scheduled. Properly installed tarps prevent further damage and protect your belongings.

Active Leak Response

Track down and stop active leaks from missing shingles, failed flashing, collapsed gutters, or tree impact damage. We work until the water intrusion is stopped and your home is dry.

Insurance Coordination

We work directly with your insurance adjuster — documenting all damage, providing itemized repair estimates, and advocating for full coverage of your legitimate claim.
