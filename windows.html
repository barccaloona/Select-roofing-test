What We Do

Windows & Doors That Perform

Old, drafty windows and doors account for up to 30% of a home's energy loss. Select Roofing installs premium, energy-efficient windows and doors that keep your home comfortable year-round while reducing utility bills.

From classic double-hung windows to sliding glass patio doors and custom entry doors, we handle the full installation, measurements, removal, installation, and finishing trim work.

Energy Star Rated
All products meet or exceed Energy Star requirements, cutting heating and cooling costs significantly.
Lifetime Warranty
Many of our window brands offer lifetime warranties on glass, frames, and hardware.
Noise Reduction
Double and triple-pane glass dramatically reduces outside noise for a quieter home.
Curb Appeal Boost
New windows and entry doors are proven to increase home value and buyer appeal at resale.
Window & Door Services

What We Offer

Window Replacement

Full removal and replacement of single, double-hung, casement, bay, and picture windows. We ensure a perfect, weathertight seal with professionally finished interior and exterior trim.

Entry Door Installation

Steel, fiberglass, and wood entry doors for every style and budget. We install single and double doors, sidelights, and transoms for a grand, welcoming entrance that's also highly secure.

Patio & Sliding Doors

Sliding glass, French, and folding patio doors that blend indoor and outdoor living. All doors feature low-E glass for maximum energy efficiency and UV protection for your flooring and furniture.

Storm Windows & Doors

Add an extra layer of protection and insulation with storm windows and storm doors. An affordable upgrade that significantly improves energy efficiency without full window replacement.
